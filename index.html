Our Mission

Tribal Elders Medical Network works to improve the health and wellbeing of Native elders throughout Skamania County. Through healthcare education, community support, and partnerships with regional healthcare providers, we help elders access services while honoring cultural identity and preserving traditional knowledge.

Hantavirus Awareness

Hantavirus is a serious illness that can spread through contact with infected rodents and their droppings. Older adults may face increased health risks, making prevention and education essential.

  • Seal gaps and openings in buildings
  • Safely clean rodent-infested areas
  • Store food securely
  • Reduce rodent access to homes
  • Seek medical attention if symptoms develop

Partnership with PeaceHealth

We collaborate with PeaceHealth and other community organizations to help Native elders access healthcare services, preventative care, and educational resources. Together we work toward reducing healthcare disparities and improving long-term health outcomes.
